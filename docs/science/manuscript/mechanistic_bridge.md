# Minimal Mechanistic Bridge for \(\gamma \approx 1\)

We keep mechanism claims minimal and falsifiable.

\gamma \approx 1
\iff
\frac{dH}{d\beta} \approx \text{const}
\]
where \(H\) is entropy-like complexity and \(\beta\) controls regime pressure.

Equivalent constructive view:

- approximately linear entropy growth in active component count,

Status: constrained hypothesis (not a full derivation).
